P0 Visual Prototypes — V1 vs V3 Comparison

Nguyễn Đức Minh Hoàng · Interactive Prototype Review

V1 — Original V3 — Interactive Cover Cards

V3 Key Improvements

  • Two-layer card system: Cover cards are now readable at 500–700px. Click any card to see the full-size detail view.
  • Contrast fix: Case Study 2 & 3 now use navy-night backgrounds instead of cream, making them stand out on the portfolio page.
  • Enlarged Hero workspace: Left panel reduced from 320px to 280px, workspace artifacts enlarged by ~12%.
  • Bold typography: Cover cards lead with large, centered titles and simplified artifact hints.
  • No new animation libraries: All hover/focus effects are pure CSS transitions.

Keyboard Accessibility

All cards are focusable with Tab. Press Enter to open the detail view. Press Escape to close the modal. The close button is also keyboard-accessible.
